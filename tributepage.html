Tribute to Srinivasa Ramanujan

Srinivasa Ramanujan (1887-1920) was an Indian mathematician who made significant contributions to mathematical analysis, number theory, and infinite series. Despite facing numerous challenges, Ramanujan independently developed many theorems and formulas, and his work has had a profound impact on several areas of mathematics.

Ramanujan's collaboration with the British mathematician G.H. Hardy played a crucial role in promoting and recognizing his work.
